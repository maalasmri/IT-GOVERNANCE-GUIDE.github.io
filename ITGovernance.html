IT Governance Model

CMMI(Capability Maturity Model Integration)

Initial

dropbox in the beginning try to find investor finding the project to run and they got financing from Startup accelerators to funding they project. In this level “work gets completed but it’s often delayed and over budget.” And In this stage the risks increase and create an unbalanced environment

Managed

in the managed focus in seven area and how to keep privacy of users of most important thing and planning the basic function in the project
1. integration management
2. scope management
3. time management
4. cost management
5. quality management
6. human resource management
7. communication management
8. risk management and
9. procurement management

Defined

: in this level dropbox is focus to growth and increase, improvement of servers Improve user experience and defining privacy policy, business agreement , terms of service , compliance.

Quantitatively managed

in this level is analyse data and statistics of the organization With the aim of defining visibility, improving user experience, and identifying risks

Optimizing

in this level the organization is stable and flexible and all foundations and rules have been laid and the company can adapt to all circumstances
